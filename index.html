Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a maintenance plan? +

If you want your website to stay secure, load fast, and work properly‚Äîyes. Websites need regular updates, security patches, and monitoring. Our plans handle all of that so you don't have to think about it.

What counts as a 'revision'? +

Minor changes like updating text, swapping images, changing business hours, adding team members, or updating prices. Major changes like new pages (except Premium's 2 free monthly pages), new features, or design overhauls are quoted separately. Premium Care includes unlimited revisions, so you don't need to worry about counting them.

What if I don't use all my revision time? +

On Starter and Professional plans, unused revision time doesn't roll over to the next month. Premium Care includes unlimited revisions, so there's no unused time to worry about. Remember‚Äîall plans cover way more than just revisions: hosting, security, backups, and support are all included.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes, with 30 days notice. We'll help you transition your hosting and files so there's no downtime.

What if I need more than my plan includes? +

No problem. Premium Care includes unlimited revisions, so you're covered there. For Starter and Professional plans, additional revision time is billed at $100/hour, or you can upgrade your plan anytime. Need new pages? Premium Care includes 2 free pages per month.

Can I add more pages to my website? +

Absolutely! Additional pages are available at $149 each for all packages. Premium Care subscribers get 2 free pages per month as part of their plan. Just let us know what pages you need and we'll add them to your site.
